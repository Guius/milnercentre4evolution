Can we use something other than what the creatures look like?

You can compare their DNA

By aligning the DNA sequences of two species, you can find out how similar their genetic code is

This gives a much more accurate indication of the relation between species

The colored lines on the mural of the Milner Centre for Evolution is a DNA alignment of eight key species
